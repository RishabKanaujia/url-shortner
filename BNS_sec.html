Right of Private Defence - BNS (Sections 37–43)

Section 37 – Right of Private Defence of the Body

Simple Meaning: Agar koi aap par aisa hamla kare jisse jaan ko khatra ho, toh aap apni suraksha ke liye uchit bal ka upyog kar sakte ho.

Example: Ramesh par raat me chaku leke koi hamla karta hai. Ramesh ne apne bachav me attacker ko zor se dhakka diya jisse vo gir gaya. Ye Private Defence ka right hai.

Section 38 – When Death Can Be Caused in Private Defence

Simple Meaning: Agar hamla aisa ho ki marne ka ya bhari chot ka dar ho, toh aap jaan le sakte ho attacker ki apni suraksha me.

Example: Koi aadmi pistol lekar aap par goli chalane wala ho, aap us par jaanleva bal ka upyog kar sakte ho.

Section 39 – Right of Private Defence of Property

Simple Meaning: Aap apni ya kisi dusre ki property (ghar, saman, zameen) ko chori, dakaiti, tod-phod se bacha sakte ho.

Example: Koi raat ko aapke ghar ka tala tod ke ghus raha ho, aap use rokhne ke liye force use kar sakte ho.

Section 40 – When Death Can Be Caused in Defence of Property

Simple Meaning: Jab dakaiti ya aag lagane jaisa khatra ho, toh jaanleva bal ka upyog property bachane me ho sakta hai.

Example: Koi aapke godown me petrol se aag lagane aa raha ho, aap jaan bachane ke liye strong action le sakte ho.
